Mr Matthew Barry

Children's Orthopaedics

Matthew Barry MBBS FRCS

Mr. Matthew Barry is an experienced Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon who was appointed in 1998 to the Royal London Hospital where he worked until October 2017.

From October 2017, Mr. Barry has worked as a children’s orthopaedic surgeon at the University Hospital Southampton, and he is happy to see children privately at the Spire Hospital in Southampton.

Experience & Specialisms

Matthew Barry has broad experience in children's orthopaedics with a particular focus on foot and ankle problems.

He also specialises in the treatment of children’s fractures and their complications including disturbances of the growth plate, limb length inequality, malalignment and non unions.
